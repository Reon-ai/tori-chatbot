# Research Sources and Technical Anchors

## openai_vector_stores

OpenAI vector stores support auto chunking; default max chunk size 800 tokens with 400 token overlap. Used to design focused Markdown files with answer-first structure.

## sans_tiling

SANS 10107 is referenced locally as a code of practice for ceramic tiling design and installation.

## porcelain

ISO/industry references classify porcelain/very low absorption dry-pressed ceramic tiles at water absorption E <= 0.5%.

## tal_large_format

TAL large-format porcelain guidance notes large-format porcelain/rectified installation, minimum grout joint guidance and levelling-system benefits.

## tal_external

TAL external surface-bed guidance supports back-buttering and limiting adhesive spread to what can be tiled before skinning.

## tal_adhesive

TAL adhesive calculation guidance: measure area, refer to bag coverage, add waste, and understand trowel/notch/site conditions affect coverage.

## tile_africa_coverage

Tile Africa gives practical adhesive coverage estimates and warns coverages are a guide only depending on site conditions, workmanship and wastage.

## abe_waterproofing

a.b.e. shower waterproofing guidance: waterproof before tiling; adhesive/grout may be water-resistant but are not waterproofing.

## sika_wet_area

Sika wet-room sequence: surface prep, waterproofing, tile setting, grouting, sealing.

## italtile_spc

Italtile/SPC installation guidance: concrete must be cured/moisture tested; moisture barriers may be required.

## finfloor_laminate

Finfloor laminate guidance: laminate reacts to humidity/moisture; installer/owner responsible for moisture barrier.

## finfloor_vinyl

Finfloor vinyl guidance includes perimeter expansion gap requirements.

## tal_floor_prep

TAL floor levelling for LVT/laminate guidance covers DPM, moisture testing and manufacturer moisture limits.

## tal_superscreed

TAL Superscreed guidance references DPM and substrate moisture content thresholds before application.

## slip_din

DIN 51130 is commonly used for shod-foot slip testing; DIN 51097 for barefoot wet areas.

## slip_r_values

R9 to R13 are ramp-test slip classifications, with higher R generally indicating higher slip resistance in that method.

## sans_plumbing

South African plumbing references include SANS 10252 Parts 1 and 2 and SANS 10400-P for drainage/plumbing context.

## sanedi_taps

SANEDI tap/flow-rate gap analysis references SANS 10252-1:2018 and SANS 1808 component standards for taps/valves.

## market_sa_tiles

Building & Decor SA flooring commentary notes ceramic/porcelain remain popular and tile sizes are increasing.

## tile_trends_2026

2026 interior tile trend sources mention natural stone looks, specialty shapes, earth/organic tones, mosaics, vertical/slim tiles and textured finishes.
