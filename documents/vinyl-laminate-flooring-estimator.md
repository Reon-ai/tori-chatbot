# Vinyl and Laminate Flooring Estimator

## Fast answer

Estimate flooring from room area plus waste, then round up to full packs and include underlay, trims, scotia and expansion accessories where required.

## Required inputs

- Room m²
- Product pack size
- Layout complexity
- Waste factor
- Underlay/moisture barrier requirement
- Skirting/scotia/trim length

## Calculation rule

Required m² = area × waste factor. Typical waste: 5–10% simple layouts, 10–15% complex layouts. Packs = required m² ÷ pack size, rounded up.

## Worked example

30 m² room with 8% waste = 32.4 m². If pack size is 2.2 m², packs = 14.73, round up to 15 packs.

## Answer format

State the estimate, show the assumptions, round up to full sellable units, and warn that final quantities depend on site conditions and product datasheets.

## Warnings

- Do not use live pack sizes unless product data confirms them.
- Do not ignore expansion gaps and moisture barrier requirements.

## Do not say or assume

- Do not guarantee the quantity as exact.
- Do not ignore product datasheets, pack sizes, site conditions or workmanship.

## Escalate to a human when

- The customer asks for a guaranteed quantity.
- The project is commercial, exterior, wet-area, pool, balcony, failed installation or legally sensitive.
- The inputs are incomplete or suspicious.
