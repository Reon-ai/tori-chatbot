RETURN & REFUND POLICY
=================================================================

We want you to love your purchase! If you're not satisfied, here's
our hassle-free return policy:

Return Window: 30 days from delivery date

Eligible Items:
• Unused items in original packaging with all tags attached
• Electronics must be returned in original box with all accessories

Non-Returnable Items:
• Items marked "Final Sale" or "Non-Returnable"
• Opened personal care items
• Downloaded digital content
• Custom/personalized items
• Gift cards

How to Return:
1. Log in to your account at www.shopbot-ai-sample.com/account
2. Go to "My Orders" and select the order
3. Click "Return Item" and select reason
4. Print the prepaid return label (free for US returns)
5. Drop off at any UPS location

Refund Timeline:
• Refund issued within 3–5 business days after we receive the item
• Credit card refunds: 5–10 business days to appear
• Store credit: Instant once item is received

Exchanges:
For exchanges, return the original item and place a new order. 
We'll process both simultaneously.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
=================================================================

Q: Do you offer price matching?
A: Yes! If you find the same item cheaper at a major US retailer
   within 14 days of purchase, we'll match the price and refund
   the difference.

Q: Can I use multiple discount codes?
A: Only one discount code can be applied per order. The best
   available discount is automatically applied.

Q: Do you ship to PO Boxes?
A: Standard shipping ships to PO Boxes. Express and Overnight
   shipping requires a physical address.

Q: What is your bulk/wholesale policy?
A: For orders of 50+ units, contact wholesale@shopbot-ai-sample.com
   for special pricing and net terms.

Q: Are your products authentic?
A: 100% — we source directly from manufacturers and authorized
   distributors. We offer a authenticity guarantee.

Q: How do I use a gift card?
A: Enter the gift card code at checkout in the "Promo/Gift Card"
   field. Multiple gift cards can be combined.

Q: What if my package shows delivered but I didn't receive it?
A: Check with neighbors and building management first. If still
   missing, contact us within 3 days of the delivery date.
   We'll open an investigation with the carrier.
