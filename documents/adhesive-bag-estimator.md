# Adhesive Bag Estimator

## Fast answer

Estimate adhesive from area, tile type, tile size, notch/trowel, substrate and bag coverage. Coverage is a guide, not a guarantee.

## Required inputs

- Area in m²
- Tile size and tile type
- Floor or wall
- Substrate condition: smooth, slightly uneven, uneven
- Notch size if known
- Bag size/coverage from datasheet

## Calculation rule

Estimated bags = area ÷ coverage per bag × substrate factor × waste factor. Suggested substrate factors: smooth 1.00, slightly uneven 1.15, uneven 1.30. Suggested waste factor: 1.10. Always use the product bag/datasheet coverage when known.

## Worked example

40 m² floor tile, coverage 3 m² per 20 kg bag, uneven substrate factor 1.30, waste 1.10: bags = 40 ÷ 3 × 1.30 × 1.10 = 19.07, so round to 20 bags.

## Answer format

State the estimate, show the assumptions, round up to full sellable units, and warn that final quantities depend on site conditions and product datasheets.

## Warnings

- Adhesive is not a levelling compound.
- Do not guarantee coverage; notch size, back-buttering and workmanship change usage.
- Use official product coverage where available.

## Do not say or assume

- Do not guarantee the quantity as exact.
- Do not ignore product datasheets, pack sizes, site conditions or workmanship.

## Escalate to a human when

- The customer asks for a guaranteed quantity.
- The project is commercial, exterior, wet-area, pool, balcony, failed installation or legally sensitive.
- The inputs are incomplete or suspicious.
