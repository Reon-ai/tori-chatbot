# Tori / Tiletoria RAG Knowledge Base v1

Generated: 2026-07-09

## What this is

This is an upload-ready Markdown knowledge base for the Tiletoria chatbot, designed for the South African market.

It covers:
- tiles and porcelain/ceramic selection
- decorative tiles and mosaics
- large-format installation risk
- adhesives, grouts, primers, waterproofing, self-levelling, spacers, clips, trims and silicone
- laminate, LVT and SPC vinyl
- baths, basins, toilets, taps, showers, wastes, traps and accessories
- calculators and worked answer formats
- contractor, homeowner, architect/designer, insurer, developer and internal-sales personas
- live data boundaries for stock, pricing, batch, shade and warranties
- QA prompts to test Tori after upload

## How to upload

Upload the entire folder structure into your chatbot's `documents` folder. Keep filenames stable and readable.

## Important

Markdown should not be the source of truth for:
- live stock
- prices
- batch/shade
- lead time
- discontinued status
- customer-specific pricing
- formal warranties

Those must come from ERP/API or authorised staff.
