# Tiletoria RAG Utility File 01: What Else Do I Need? Add-On Shopping Lists

This RAG-ready file helps the chatbot answer the customer question: “What else do I need with this product?” It is designed for South African retail customers buying tiles, glue-down Luxury Vinyl Tile, sanitaryware, taps, baths, showers and related installation products.

Source basis: tile installation principles from TCNA/ANSI-style guidance, levelling-system manufacturer guidance, resilient flooring installation guidance, and reputable bathroom-system catalogue logic. Exact compatibility, product inclusion and coverage must always be confirmed against the specific Tiletoria product, box contents, data sheet and installer requirements.

Important chatbot rule: Add-ons must be positioned as practical compatibility checks, not pressure selling. The chatbot should say “please confirm” rather than “you must buy” unless the accessory is clearly required for the product to function.

## Master Add-On Principle

Summary: Most renovation products are part of a system, so customers often need accessories, consumables or compatible fittings in addition to the visible product.

Core rule: The main product is often only one part of the installation.

Customer-friendly wording: “To avoid installation delays, please confirm the matching accessories and installation products before your installer arrives.”

Commercial wording: “Tiletoria can help you check the full installation set so the product is ready to install correctly.”

Never imply: The customer must buy every possible accessory.

Always imply: The customer should confirm which accessories are needed for their specific product, site and installer method.

Escalate when: The product involves water pressure, concealed plumbing, waterproofing, exterior use, shower areas, ramps, commercial installations, large-format tiles, damp substrates or unclear product compatibility.

## Floor Tile Shopping Checklist

Summary: Floor tiles normally require adhesive, grout, spacers, trims and cleaning products, with additional products depending on tile size and substrate condition.

Main product: Floor tiles.

Common add-ons:
Adhesive: suitable tile adhesive matched to tile type, size, substrate and area.
Primer: required for some substrates or preparation systems.
Grout: correct grout type and colour.
Spacers: selected for the required grout joint width.
Wedge-and-clip levelling system: useful for many large-format tile installations.
Trims: required for exposed edges, transitions, steps or external corners.
Movement joint material: required where movement joints or perimeter gaps must be maintained.
Grout haze remover: useful after cement-based grouting where compatible.
Neutral tile cleaner: useful for routine maintenance.
Spare tiles: recommended from the same shade and calibre batch.

Chatbot answer: “For floor tiles, please also check adhesive, grout, spacers, trims, possible levelling clips, grout haze remover and a suitable cleaner. Your installer should confirm the adhesive type, grout joint width and whether levelling clips are needed.”

Escalation rule: Escalate for large-format tiles, exterior floors, ramps, heated floors, cracked screeds, wet areas or commercial spaces.
