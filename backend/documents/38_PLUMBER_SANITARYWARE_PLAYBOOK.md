# Plumber and Sanitaryware Playbook

**Version:** 2026-06-18  
**Market:** South Africa / Southern Africa  
**Business:** Tiletoria customer and staff RAG knowledge base  

## Purpose
Guide Tori on plumber-focused questions for toilets, basins, mixers, wastes, traps and bathroom compatibility.

## Retrieval triggers
- plumber
- toilet
- pan
- cistern
- basin
- mixer
- tap
- waste
- trap
- sanitaryware
- bathroom fitting

## Persona profile
Plumbers and bathroom renovators need compatibility, dimensions and installation constraints. Tori must not invent fitment dimensions for specific products unless the official product sheet is in the knowledge base.

## Ask for
- Product name/code.
- Existing plumbing layout.
- New build or renovation?
- Wall-hung, close-coupled, back-to-wall or freestanding requirement?
- Water inlet and waste position.
- Space available.
- Photos where possible.
- Need technical sheet?

## Toilets
Tori must check:
- Pan type.
- P-trap or S-trap requirement where relevant.
- Set-out distance if floor outlet.
- Cistern type.
- Seat compatibility.
- Space around pan.

## Basins
Check:
- Countertop, wall-hung, inset, undermount, pedestal or vanity basin.
- Tap hole requirement.
- Waste type.
- Trap visibility.
- Cabinet/vanity compatibility.

## Mixers and taps
Check:
- Basin, bath, shower, kitchen or bidet application.
- Wall-mounted or deck-mounted.
- Pressure compatibility where known.
- Finish colour.
- Matching accessories.

## Wastes and traps
Tori should not guarantee compatibility without product details. Ask for basin/bath/shower type and photos.

## Safe plumber answer
"Please send the product code or photo and the existing plumbing layout. The team can confirm the correct technical sheet and compatibility before you install."

## Red flags
Escalate when:
- Product must fit existing concealed plumbing.
- Wall-hung toilet frame needed.
- Waterproofing around shower/bath is involved.
- Customer asks for pressure guarantee.
- Commercial/public bathroom compliance is involved.

## Universal Tori rule for this file
Tori must be helpful, practical and commercially safe. Tori may guide, calculate and explain, but must not invent live stock, live prices, delivery availability, technical certifications, warranties, legal outcomes or product suitability where site conditions are unknown. When the question depends on site inspection, current branch stock, quoted pricing, account status or formal compliance, Tori must capture the correct details and route to the correct Tiletoria team.
