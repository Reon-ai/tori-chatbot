# Sanitaryware, Tapware and Plumbing Guide

## Purpose

Use this file for toilets, basins, baths, mixers, showers and plumbing-related product questions.

Tori can guide product selection, but a plumber must confirm site compatibility.

---

# Toilets

## Key Checks

- Close-coupled, back-to-wall, wall-hung or other type.
- Waste outlet position.
- Water inlet position.
- Cistern type.
- Seat included or excluded.
- Pan connector requirements.
- Space from wall.
- Floor level.
- Existing plumbing.
- Building/body corporate rules.

## Safe Answer

> Toilet replacement depends on waste position, water inlet, available space and the type of toilet. Please ask your plumber to confirm compatibility before purchase.

## Escalate

Always escalate wall-hung toilets and concealed cisterns.

---

# Basins

## Key Checks

- Countertop, wall-hung, inset, semi-recessed or pedestal.
- Tap hole or no tap hole.
- Mixer height.
- Waste type.
- Trap type.
- Vanity compatibility.
- Wall support.
- Space and splash zone.

## Safe Answer

> Basin choice depends on the vanity, tap position, waste and trap. Please confirm measurements and plumbing with your plumber.

---

# Baths

## Key Checks

- Freestanding, built-in or back-to-wall.
- Waste position.
- Mixer type.
- Floor support.
- Access for plumbing.
- Overflow.
- Bath size and room access.
- Waterproofing around bath area.

## Safe Answer

> Bath selection depends on space, waste position, mixer choice and plumbing access. Freestanding baths especially need careful set-out before purchase.

---

# Basin Mixers

## Key Checks

- Basin type.
- Tap hole.
- Spout reach.
- Mixer height.
- Water pressure.
- Finish care.
- Pop-up waste compatibility.

## Safe Answer

> Please match the basin mixer to the basin type and ask your plumber to confirm water pressure, spout reach and waste compatibility.

---

# Shower Mixers

## Key Checks

- Exposed or concealed.
- Single outlet or multiple outlets.
- Diverter needed.
- Water pressure.
- Hot water capacity.
- Wall depth.
- Service access.
- Waterproofing around penetrations.

## Safe Answer

> Shower mixers should be confirmed before tiling because concealed plumbing, wall depth, outlet positions and waterproofing all matter.

## Escalate

Always escalate concealed mixers, thermostatic mixers, multi-outlet showers and low-pressure questions.

---

# Rain Shower Heads

## Key Checks

- Shower head size.
- Water pressure.
- Flow rate.
- Hot water capacity.
- Ceiling or wall outlet.
- Mixer compatibility.
- Cleaning access.

## Safe Answer

> A large rain shower head may need more flow and pressure than a small shower head. Please confirm pressure and flow with your plumber.

---

# Black, Brass and Coloured Finishes

## Key Checks

- Cleaning instructions.
- Water quality.
- Limescale risk.
- Abrasive cleaners.
- Coastal conditions.
- Matching accessories.
- Warranty terms.

## Safe Answer

> Special finishes such as black or brushed brass need gentle cleaning and proper care. Avoid abrasive pads, harsh chemicals and cleaners not approved for the finish.

---

# Plumber Handover Checklist

Ask the plumber to confirm:

- Water pressure.
- Hot water system.
- Waste positions.
- Inlet positions.
- Wall depth.
- Fixing support.
- Mixer compatibility.
- Trap/waste requirements.
- Waterproofing around penetrations.
- Access for future maintenance.
- Body corporate or municipal constraints where relevant.

## Safe CTA

> Before buying, send your plumber the product size/specification and ask them to confirm compatibility with your existing plumbing.
