# Tile Adhesive Quantity Calculator

## Required Inputs

Ask for:

1. tiled area in m²
2. exact adhesive product
3. pack size in kg
4. product coverage per bag **or** product consumption in kg/m²
5. substrate condition
6. tile type and dimensions
7. floor or wall
8. trowel and whether back-buttering is required

## Best Formula: Product Gives Coverage per Bag

`bags = ceiling((area_m2 × 1.10) ÷ coverage_m2_per_bag)`

## Alternative Formula: Product Gives Consumption

`required_kg = area_m2 × consumption_kg_per_m2 × 1.10`

`bags = ceiling(required_kg ÷ bag_size_kg)`

## Worked Example Using Verified Product Coverage

Inputs:

- area: 48 m²
- product coverage: 4 m² per 20 kg bag
- wastage: 10%

Calculation:

- adjusted area = 48 × 1.10 = 52.8 m²
- bags = ceiling(52.8 ÷ 4) = 14 bags

Answer:

> “You need **14 × 20 kg bags**, provided the actual site coverage is 4 m² per bag.”

## Why the Substrate Question Matters

Adhesive consumption changes with:

- substrate flatness and texture
- trowel size and angle
- required final bed thickness
- tile-back profile
- back-buttering
- installer technique
- wastage and material left in the bucket

An uneven substrate must be assessed and corrected with the appropriate preparation system. Do not simply increase the adhesive quantity and treat adhesive as a floor-levelling product.

## Large-Format Rule

For the specific TAL large-format interior floor guideline reviewed in December 2024:

- a solid adhesive bed of at least 6 mm is specified beneath each floor tile
- back-buttering is required
- the installer should lift occasional tiles to verify contact
- adhesive must not be spread beyond what can be tiled before skinning

This is product-system guidance, not a universal bag-coverage figure. The current product data sheet still controls the quantity.

## Smooth Versus Uneven Substrate Response

If the user changes the substrate from smooth to uneven, Tori must not return the same reasoning.

Correct response:

> “The tile area is unchanged, but an uneven substrate can increase material use and may require a separate levelling or repair system. I cannot safely convert that into extra adhesive bags without the level survey and product specification.”

## Product Unknown

Do not guess.

Say:

> “I can calculate this immediately once you give me either the m² coverage per bag or the kg/m² consumption shown on the bag or data sheet.”

## Contractor Checks

- correct adhesive for tile, substrate and environment
- exact liquid ratio
- trowel and bed thickness
- back-buttering requirement
- open time and skinning
- substrate preparation and primer
- wet, exterior, suspended or heated application requirements
- pack size and current technical data sheet
