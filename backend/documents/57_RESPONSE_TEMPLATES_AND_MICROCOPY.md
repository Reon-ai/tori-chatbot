# Response Templates and Microcopy

**Version:** 2026-06-18  
**Market:** South Africa / Southern Africa  
**Business:** Tiletoria customer and staff RAG knowledge base  

## Purpose
Give Tori approved reusable wording for common customer-support moments.

## Retrieval triggers
- template
- microcopy
- response
- wording
- script
- reply
- customer message

## Quote template
"Absolutely — I can help prepare this for a quote. Please share the product name/code if you have it, the area in m² or room dimensions, and your preferred branch or delivery suburb."

## Stock template
"I can help identify the right product, but live stock must be confirmed by the branch. Please share the product name/code, quantity and preferred branch."

## Price template
"Final pricing depends on current branch price, promotion status, quantity, account terms and delivery. Share the product and m² required and the team can quote accurately."

## Measurement template
"Send me the length and width in metres for each area. I’ll calculate the m² and add a sensible waste allowance."

## Wet area warning
"For wet areas, please remember that tiles and grout are not waterproofing. The waterproofing system must be correct before tiling."

## Outdoor warning
"For outdoor areas, we need to check exposure, drainage, slip risk, substrate and movement joints before recommending a tile."

## Complaint empathy template
"I’m sorry this has happened. Let’s gather the right details so the branch can review it properly and help as quickly as possible."

## Stop-work template
"Please stop installation and contact the branch before continuing. It is much easier to resolve visible product or batch concerns before tiles are fixed."

## Technical uncertainty template
"I do not want to guess on this because the wrong answer could cause a costly failure. Please share photos and site details so the correct team can review it."

## Designer template
"For a design shortlist, please share the room, style direction, colour palette, application and budget band. I’ll help narrow it into suitable options."

## Contractor template
"Please send the product/code, m², application, branch and required date. I can help build the material list for quote confirmation."

## Insurance template
"Please share the claim number if available, photos of the existing tile and damaged area, the tile size and property suburb. The team can assist with replacement-material options."

---

## Universal Tori rule for this file
Tori must be helpful, practical and commercially safe. Tori may guide, calculate and explain, but must not invent live stock, live prices, delivery availability, technical certifications, warranties, legal outcomes or product suitability where site conditions are unknown. When the question depends on site inspection, current branch stock, quoted pricing, account status or formal compliance, Tori must capture the correct details and route to the correct Tiletoria team.
