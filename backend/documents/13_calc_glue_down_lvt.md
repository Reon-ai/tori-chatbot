# Glue-Down LVT Quantity and Adhesive Calculator

## Product Scope

Tiletoria's current calculator guidance covers **glue-down LVT**. Do not answer as though the product is click vinyl or laminate unless a future verified product file says so.

## LVT Pack Quantity

Required inputs:

- net floor area
- pack coverage in m²
- approved waste; default 10%

Formula:

`order_area = net_area × 1.10`

`packs = ceiling(order_area ÷ pack_coverage)`

## LVT Adhesive Quantity

Use the exact adhesive and trowel coverage.

Formula:

`adhesive_units = ceiling(order_area ÷ coverage_per_unit)`

## Verified TAL Profix Plus LVT Planning Coverage

Published approximate coverage:

- 1.6 mm V-notch trowel: about 0.63 kg/m², or about 10.9 m² per 5 L kit
- 3 mm V-notch trowel: about 0.98 kg/m², or about 7.0 m² per 5 L kit

Actual coverage depends on site conditions and workmanship.

## Worked Example

Inputs:

- net area: 55 m²
- wastage: 10%
- 5 L kit coverage: 10.9 m²

Calculation:

- order area = 55 × 1.10 = 60.5 m²
- kits = ceiling(60.5 ÷ 10.9) = 6 kits

Answer:

> “Allow **6 × 5 L kits** at the published 1.6 mm V-notch coverage.”

## Site Questions Before Finalising

Ask about:

- substrate type
- moisture testing
- smoothness and level
- cracks and joints
- existing coatings or adhesive
- required smoothing compound
- product acclimatisation
- installation temperature
- direction and layout
- skirting, trims and transitions
- rolling requirements
- traffic and handover timing

## Hard Rules

- Glue-down LVT needs a properly prepared, smooth, sound and dry substrate.
- Do not use adhesive quantity to compensate for surface irregularities.
- Use the current LVT and adhesive installation instructions.
- Resilient flooring installation is skilled work; route technical uncertainty to an experienced installer or technical consultant.
