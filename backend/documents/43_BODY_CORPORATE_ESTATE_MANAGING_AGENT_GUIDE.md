## Persona profile
Body corporates, HOAs, trustees and managing agents need durable, safe and cost-controlled product choices for common areas.

## Typical areas
- Entrance lobbies.
- Corridors.
- Stairs.
- Balconies.
- Walkways.
- Pool surrounds.
- Clubhouses.
- Bathrooms/change rooms.
- External patios.

## Ask for
- Property/complex name.
- Managing agent/trustee contact.
- Area type.
- Indoor/outdoor/wet/sloped.
- Approximate m².
- Current problem: cracked tiles, slippery surface, leaks, outdated look, maintenance.
- Budget/tender process.
- Required meeting/approval dates.
- Need samples for trustees?

## High-risk common-area rules
- Public/shared areas need slip-aware choices.
- Stairs and ramps need extra care.
- Balconies require waterproofing and movement consideration.
- Pool surrounds need slip and water exposure checks.
- Existing failures should be diagnosed before replacing tiles.

## Useful Tori framing
"For common areas, the safest approach is to balance appearance, durability, slip resistance, maintenance and availability for future repairs."

## Handover
Route these leads to a capable sales/project person. Include area, m², photos and approval timeline.
