# Showroom Sales Scripts for Staff Learning

**Version:** 2026-06-18  
**Market:** South Africa / Southern Africa  
**Business:** Tiletoria customer and staff RAG knowledge base  

## Purpose
Provide staff-friendly scripts Tori can use as a learning tool for showroom and sales teams.

## Tori as staff coach
Tori should help staff guide customers through a professional journey, not just answer product questions.

## Opening script
"Welcome. Are you looking for tiles or finishes for a specific room or project?"

## Qualification questions
- Which area are you working on?
- Is it floor, wall, wet area or outdoor?
- Are you renovating or building new?
- Do you already have measurements?
- What style are you going for?
- What budget range should we work within?
- Do you need delivery?
- Who is installing?

## Needs-based selling sequence
1. Application.
2. Style.
3. Size/format.
4. Technical suitability.
5. Quantity.
6. Installation system.
7. Delivery/collection.
8. Quote and follow-up.

## Cross-sell prompts
When selling tiles, remember:
- Adhesive.
- Grout.
- Spacers/levelling clips.
- Trims/profiles.
- Waterproofing.
- Primer/self-levelling.
- Cleaner/maintenance products.
- Extra box for future repairs.

## Dangerous shortcut script
If customer wants to buy tile only:
"We can quote the tile only, but I recommend we also check the installation products so the tile is installed correctly. The wrong adhesive or missing waterproofing can cost far more later."

## Closing script
"Let’s turn this into a proper quote with the correct quantities, extras and branch stock check."

## Follow-up script
"Hi, just following up on your Tiletoria quote. Do you need help checking quantities, delivery or matching adhesive/grout before you decide?"

## Staff behaviour standard
- Ask before recommending.
- Do not sell outdoor/wet/commercial by appearance only.
- Protect the customer from underbuying.
- Protect Tiletoria from overpromising.

---

## Universal Tori rule for this file
Tori must be helpful, practical and commercially safe. Tori may guide, calculate and explain, but must not invent live stock, live prices, delivery availability, technical certifications, warranties, legal outcomes or product suitability where site conditions are unknown. When the question depends on site inspection, current branch stock, quoted pricing, account status or formal compliance, Tori must capture the correct details and route to the correct Tiletoria team.
