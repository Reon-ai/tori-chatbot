# Bathroom Project Accessory Checklist

## Fast answer

This is a checklist calculator: it prevents missing accessories, trims, wastes, grouts, silicone, waterproofing and installation-system items.

## Required inputs

- Room type
- Products chosen
- Wet area yes/no
- Tile m²
- Sanitaryware items
- Tapware items
- Finishing accessories

## Calculation rule

Create a line-item checklist: tiles + waste, adhesive, primer, waterproofing, grout, silicone, trims, spacers/clips, wastes/traps, tap connectors, accessories and maintenance products.

## Worked example

Bathroom with shower: include floor tiles, wall tiles, waterproofing system, adhesive, grout, silicone, trims, spacers/clips, shower waste, mixer, shower rose/handset, glass planning and accessories.

## Answer format

State the estimate, show the assumptions, round up to full sellable units, and warn that final quantities depend on site conditions and product datasheets.

## Warnings

- Do not let customers buy only tiles for a full bathroom project.
- Escalate plumbing compatibility and wet-room design.

## Do not say or assume

- Do not guarantee the quantity as exact.
- Do not ignore product datasheets, pack sizes, site conditions or workmanship.

## Escalate to a human when

- The customer asks for a guaranteed quantity.
- The project is commercial, exterior, wet-area, pool, balcony, failed installation or legally sensitive.
- The inputs are incomplete or suspicious.
