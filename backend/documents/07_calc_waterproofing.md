# Waterproofing Quantity Calculator

## Required Inputs

Ask for:

1. floor length and width
2. total wall lengths to be waterproofed
3. waterproofing height
4. upstands, niches, benches, plinths, kerbs and returns
5. exact waterproofing product
6. stated coverage for the completed system
7. kit or bucket size
8. membrane roll width and length where required

## Calculate the Treated Area

Floor:

`floor_area = length × width`

Walls:

`wall_area = total_wall_length × waterproofing_height`

Add other treated surfaces separately.

`net_waterproof_area = floor_area + wall_area + niches + benches + upstands + returns`

`order_area = net_waterproof_area × 1.10`

## Product Quantity

If the product states completed-system coverage:

`kits = ceiling(order_area ÷ product_coverage_per_kit)`

Do not multiply by the number of coats when the published coverage already refers to the final specified film thickness.

## Verified Product Examples

### TAL Sureproof

- approximate coverage: 10–12 m² per 20 kg kit
- published coverage is at the specified cured film
- for conservative planning, use 10 m² per kit unless the current data sheet says otherwise

### TAL Sureproof Shower

- approximate coverage: 3 m² per 5 kg bucket
- published cured film thickness: 1.5–2.0 mm

## Worked Shower Example

Inputs:

- floor: 1.2 × 0.9 m = 1.08 m²
- total wall length: 4.2 m
- waterproofing height: 1.8 m
- wall area: 4.2 × 1.8 = 7.56 m²
- net area: 8.64 m²
- 10% allowance: 9.50 m²
- TAL Sureproof Shower coverage: 3 m² per 5 kg bucket

Quantity:

`ceiling(9.50 ÷ 3) = 4 buckets`

Answer:

> “You need **4 × 5 kg buckets**, before adding any separately measured niches, benches or extra returns.”

## Waterproofing Is a System

Quantity alone is not enough. Confirm:

- substrate type and moisture limit
- primer or keying system
- required reinforcing membrane
- corners, changes of plane and penetrations
- drain or puddle-flange detail
- coats and cured film thickness
- overlaps
- drying and curing
- flood testing
- protection from following trades
- compatible adhesive and grout system

## Hard Rules

- Tile, adhesive and grout do not replace waterproofing.
- Do not calculate shower walls from floor area.
- Do not omit niches, benches, kerbs, plinths, pipe penetrations or drain detailing.
- Do not invent the required waterproofing height.
- Balcony, pool, roof deck, suspended slab and commercial wet-area systems require technical confirmation.
