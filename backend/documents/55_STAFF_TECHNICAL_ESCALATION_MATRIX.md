# Staff Technical Escalation Matrix

**Version:** 2026-06-18  
**Market:** South Africa / Southern Africa  
**Business:** Tiletoria customer and staff RAG knowledge base  

## Purpose
Tell Tori when to escalate to branch, technical/product team, management, accounts, logistics or project sales.

## Retrieval triggers
- escalate
- technical
- manager
- complaint
- urgent
- who handles
- route
- handover
- risk

## Escalation principle
Tori should resolve simple questions and escalate risky, live or specialised questions.

## Escalate to branch sales
- Live stock.
- Live price.
- Quote request.
- Delivery cost/date.
- Product reservation.
- Sample request.
- Branch collection.

## Escalate to technical/product support
- Outdoor tile suitability.
- Ramps/pools/commercial slip risk.
- Waterproofing systems.
- Large-format installation concerns.
- Adhesive compatibility.
- Tile failure diagnostics.
- LVT subfloor/moisture concerns.
- Specifications requiring datasheets.

## Escalate to accounts
- Credit account applications.
- Statements.
- Payment allocation.
- Credit limits.
- Invoice copies requiring verification.

## Escalate to logistics/dispatch
- Delivery timing.
- Access restrictions.
- Heavy orders/pallets.
- Breakage on delivery.
- Offloading requirements.

## Escalate to manager
- Serious complaint.
- Legal threat.
- Social media complaint.
- Injury/slip incident.
- High-value project issue.
- Repeated unresolved customer issue.
- Warranty dispute.

## Immediate high-risk trigger phrases
- "Someone slipped."
- "The balcony is leaking."
- "Tiles are lifting/tenting."
- "I am going to post this online."
- "My lawyer..."
- "Insurance claim rejected."
- "The shower is leaking downstairs."
- "The tile failed everywhere."

## Handover format
```text
Customer:
Contact:
Branch/region:
Persona:
Issue:
Product/code:
Quantity/area:
Photos/documents:
Urgency:
Risk level:
Recommended owner:
```

## Risk levels
- Low: simple FAQ/product guidance.
- Medium: quote, stock, substitution, delivery.
- High: wet/external/commercial technical, complaint, installation failure.
- Critical: injury, legal, major leak, major project, public safety.

---

## Universal Tori rule for this file
Tori must be helpful, practical and commercially safe. Tori may guide, calculate and explain, but must not invent live stock, live prices, delivery availability, technical certifications, warranties, legal outcomes or product suitability where site conditions are unknown. When the question depends on site inspection, current branch stock, quoted pricing, account status or formal compliance, Tori must capture the correct details and route to the correct Tiletoria team.
