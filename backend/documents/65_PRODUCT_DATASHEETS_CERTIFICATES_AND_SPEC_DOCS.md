# Product Datasheets, Certificates and Specification Documents

**Version:** 2026-06-18  
**Market:** South Africa / Southern Africa  
**Business:** Tiletoria customer and staff RAG knowledge base  

## Purpose
Help Tori route technical document requests for architects, developers, insurers and commercial customers.

## Retrieval triggers
- datasheet
- data sheet
- certificate
- technical sheet
- spec sheet
- test report
- COC
- R rating
- slip certificate
- compliance

## Document request rule
Tori may collect the request, but must not invent datasheet values or certificates.

## Ask for
- Product name/code.
- Required document type.
- Project/application.
- Who requires it: architect, insurer, developer, contractor, council, client?
- Deadline.
- Branch/contact details.

## Common requested documents
- Product datasheet.
- Slip-resistance information.
- Technical specification.
- Maintenance guidance.
- Warranty information.
- Installation product datasheets.
- Supplier/manufacturer certificate where available.

## Tori wording
"Please share the product code and the document you need. The team can confirm whether the current datasheet or certificate is available for that exact product and batch/range."

## High-risk document requests
Escalate for:
- Ramps.
- Public areas.
- Commercial kitchens.
- Pools.
- Healthcare/education/public-sector projects.
- Insurance disputes.
- Legal claims.

## Do not
Do not claim that a product complies with a spec unless the exact documentation is available and approved.

---

## Universal Tori rule for this file
Tori must be helpful, practical and commercially safe. Tori may guide, calculate and explain, but must not invent live stock, live prices, delivery availability, technical certifications, warranties, legal outcomes or product suitability where site conditions are unknown. When the question depends on site inspection, current branch stock, quoted pricing, account status or formal compliance, Tori must capture the correct details and route to the correct Tiletoria team.
