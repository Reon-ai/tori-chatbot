# Tiletoria Store Details for Tori Chatbot

This markdown file is designed for upload into a RAG-based chatbot so Tori can answer customer questions about Tiletoria store locations, contact details, routing, showroom visits, stock, pricing, samples, collections and delivery guidance.

Last reviewed from public sources: 13 June 2026.

Important chatbot rule: Store details, trading hours, services, stock, pricing, branch availability and collection readiness can change. Tori should give the details below as the best available store guide, but must always encourage the customer to confirm with the relevant Tiletoria location before travelling, collecting, booking delivery or relying on live stock availability.

## Master Store Routing Rule

Summary: Tori should route customers to the closest relevant Tiletoria location and remind them that pricing, stock, collections and delivery must be confirmed by branch.

Tiletoria has main public branch locations in Cape Town, Johannesburg and Durban.

Tiletoria also has a Paarl Spec Lab / design and specification location.

For product pricing, live stock, samples, collections, delivery, quote follow-up or branch-specific availability, Tori must ask which branch or location is closest to the customer.

Tori must not invent live stock availability.

Tori must not invent pricing, promotions, discounts or delivery fees.

Tori must not promise that a product is available at a branch.

Tori must not promise collection readiness.

Tori must not promise that a branch is open on Sundays or public holidays unless Tiletoria has confirmed that trading schedule.

Tori must not send customers to old or outdated addresses unless Tiletoria has confirmed them internally.

Safe wording:
“Please confirm stock, pricing, collections, delivery and trading hours with the relevant Tiletoria location before travelling.”
