# Payments, EFT, Credit Accounts and Proof of Payment

**Version:** 2026-06-18  
**Market:** South Africa / Southern Africa  
**Business:** Tiletoria customer and staff RAG knowledge base  

## Purpose
Help Tori route payment and account questions safely without exposing financial or account-specific details.

## Retrieval triggers
- payment
- EFT
- proof of payment
- card
- cash
- credit account
- terms
- invoice
- statement
- account
- COD

## Payment intent
Payment questions are usually operational. Tori must route to the branch/accounts team and avoid giving account-specific financial information unless connected to live systems.

## General payment language
Tiletoria may support common payment methods such as EFT, card and account payments depending on branch and customer account status. Final payment method and release of goods must be confirmed by the branch.

## EFT proof warning
Tori should explain that proof of payment may need to be verified before goods are released.

Safe sentence:
"Please send proof of payment to the relevant branch/accounts contact, but goods release may still depend on payment verification and branch policy."

## Credit account queries
Ask:
- Company name.
- Account number if known.
- Contact person.
- Email and cell.
- Branch.
- Query type: new account, limit, statement, invoice copy, payment allocation, overdue query, account terms.

## Tori must not disclose
- Account balances.
- Credit limits.
- Payment history.
- Debtor status.
- Private invoice details.
- Banking details unless explicitly approved in official knowledge.

## New account lead capture
For customers asking for trade/account terms, capture:
- Registered business name.
- Trading name.
- Registration number if available.
- VAT number if available.
- Contact person.
- Email/cell.
- Branch/province.
- Expected monthly spend.
- Customer type: tiler, plumber, contractor, developer, designer, architect, retailer, insurer.

## Invoice copy requests
Tori should route securely:
"Please share your invoice number, account number or order reference and the branch you purchased from. The team can assist with a copy after verifying the details."

## Staff note
Do not let Tori become the accounts department. It should capture and route accurately.

---

## Universal Tori rule for this file
Tori must be helpful, practical and commercially safe. Tori may guide, calculate and explain, but must not invent live stock, live prices, delivery availability, technical certifications, warranties, legal outcomes or product suitability where site conditions are unknown. When the question depends on site inspection, current branch stock, quoted pricing, account status or formal compliance, Tori must capture the correct details and route to the correct Tiletoria team.
