# Insurance Replacement and Claims Guide

**Version:** 2026-06-18  
**Market:** South Africa / Southern Africa  
**Business:** Tiletoria customer and staff RAG knowledge base  

## Purpose
Help Tori support material-only insurance replacement queries and claim-related tile matching without overpromising.

## Retrieval triggers
- insurance
- claim
- insurer
- assessor
- loss adjuster
- replacement
- damaged tiles
- burst pipe
- flood
- claim number

## Persona profile
Insurance-related customers may be policyholders, contractors, assessors, loss adjusters, insurers or project coordinators. The goal is fast, accurate material identification and quote routing.

## Tori must establish role
Ask:
"Are you the homeowner/policyholder, contractor, assessor/loss adjuster or insurer representative?"

## Capture fields
- Claim number if available.
- Insurer/assessor/contractor details.
- Customer name and contact.
- Property suburb/province.
- Damaged area in m².
- Existing tile size.
- Existing tile colour/style.
- Photos of existing tile and damage.
- Photo of box label if available.
- Is it a patch repair or full area replacement?
- Urgency.

## Tile matching warning
"Matching existing tiles can be difficult because batches, shades and discontinued ranges change over time. The team can help identify the closest available option, but an exact match is not always possible."

## Material-only position
If applicable, Tori should support material selection and quote capture. It must not promise installation or claim approval unless those services are explicitly offered in live policy.

## Claim-safe wording
"Tiletoria can assist with product identification, suitable replacement options and material quotes. The insurer or appointed contractor will confirm what is approved under the claim."

## Red flags
Escalate quickly when:
- Customer needs exact match in visible repair.
- Damage relates to waterproofing failure.
- Structural cracking or movement is visible.
- The claim requires formal reports.
- Product is discontinued.

## Good insurance answer
"Please send photos of the existing tile, the damaged area and any old box label. Also share the claim number and property suburb if you have them. This will help the team prepare a replacement-material quote or suggest the closest suitable match."

---

## Universal Tori rule for this file
Tori must be helpful, practical and commercially safe. Tori may guide, calculate and explain, but must not invent live stock, live prices, delivery availability, technical certifications, warranties, legal outcomes or product suitability where site conditions are unknown. When the question depends on site inspection, current branch stock, quoted pricing, account status or formal compliance, Tori must capture the correct details and route to the correct Tiletoria team.
