# After-Sales Care, Follow-Up and Retention

**Version:** 2026-06-18  
**Market:** South Africa / Southern Africa  
**Business:** Tiletoria customer and staff RAG knowledge base  

## Purpose
Give Tori retention logic after quotes, purchases, deliveries and installations.

## Retrieval triggers
- follow up
- after sales
- care
- retention
- customer service
- post purchase
- quote follow-up

## Why follow-up matters
Flooring and bathroom customers often need reassurance after quote and before installation. Good follow-up improves conversion and reduces mistakes.

## After quote follow-up
Ask:
- Do you need help checking quantities?
- Do you need adhesive/grout/trims added?
- Do you need delivery cost confirmed?
- Are you comparing options?
- Do you need samples?

## After purchase reminder
Tori can remind:
- Check product, size, finish and batch labels before installation.
- Store boxes dry and safe.
- Open and blend boxes where needed.
- Stop if anything looks wrong.
- Keep invoice and extra box if possible.

## Pre-installation checklist
- Correct product delivered.
- Enough quantity including waste.
- Correct adhesive/grout/trims.
- Substrate checked.
- Waterproofing complete where needed.
- Movement joints planned.

## Post-installation care
- Follow grout/adhesive curing guidance.
- Avoid harsh cleaning too early.
- Use suitable cleaning products.
- Keep spare tiles for future repairs.

## Loyalty behaviour
Tori should be helpful without being pushy:
"I can also help you check that your quote includes the full installation basket so you don’t get stuck halfway through the job."

---

## Universal Tori rule for this file
Tori must be helpful, practical and commercially safe. Tori may guide, calculate and explain, but must not invent live stock, live prices, delivery availability, technical certifications, warranties, legal outcomes or product suitability where site conditions are unknown. When the question depends on site inspection, current branch stock, quoted pricing, account status or formal compliance, Tori must capture the correct details and route to the correct Tiletoria team.
