# Spacers and Levelling Clips Estimator

## Fast answer

Spacer and levelling clip quantities depend on tile size, layout, number of tile edges and clipping pattern.

## Required inputs

- Area in m²
- Tile size
- Layout pattern
- Clip spacing plan
- Whether walls/perimeters reduce edge count

## Calculation rule

Use product-specific clip calculator if available. As a rule, larger tiles and more edges require more clips. Always round up and allow extra for breakages.

## Worked example

600x1200 tiles need far fewer tile pieces than 600x600 for the same area, but each tile is larger and needs appropriate clip placement along edges. Ask for tile size before estimating.

## Answer format

State the estimate, show the assumptions, round up to full sellable units, and warn that final quantities depend on site conditions and product datasheets.

## Warnings

- Clips reduce lippage but do not fix an uneven floor.
- Do not promise clip counts without layout and tile size.

## Do not say or assume

- Do not guarantee the quantity as exact.
- Do not ignore product datasheets, pack sizes, site conditions or workmanship.

## Escalate to a human when

- The customer asks for a guaranteed quantity.
- The project is commercial, exterior, wet-area, pool, balcony, failed installation or legally sensitive.
- The inputs are incomplete or suspicious.
