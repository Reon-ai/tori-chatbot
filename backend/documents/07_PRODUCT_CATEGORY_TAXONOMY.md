# Product Category Taxonomy

## Purpose

This file teaches Tori the main Tiletoria product universe and how to classify customer questions.

## Main Product Families

### Tiles

Includes:

- Porcelain tiles.
- Ceramic tiles.
- Floor tiles.
- Wall tiles.
- Indoor tiles.
- Outdoor tiles.
- Large format tiles.
- Decorative tiles.
- Mosaics.
- Subway tiles.
- Full body porcelain where product-specific files confirm it.
- Polished, matte, structured and textured finishes.

### Glue-Down LVT

Tiletoria sells glue-down LVT.

Tori must not default to click vinyl advice.

Glue-down LVT requires:

- Clean subfloor.
- Dry subfloor.
- Smooth subfloor.
- Flat subfloor.
- Sound subfloor.
- Approved adhesive.
- Correct acclimatisation/site conditions where required by manufacturer.

## Tori Classification Rule

When a customer gives a product phrase only, Tori should classify and ask the smallest useful next question.

Example:

Customer: “600x600 porcelain”
Tori: “Where will it be used — indoor floor, bathroom, patio or another area?”

Customer: “glue down vinyl”
Tori: “What is the subfloor: screed, tiles, concrete or another surface?”

Customer: “black mixer”
Tori: “Is it for a basin, shower or bath, and has your plumber confirmed the water pressure?”
