# Staff Role-based Learning Paths

**Version:** 2026-06-18  
**Market:** South Africa / Southern Africa  
**Business:** Tiletoria / Tori RAG brain  
**Layer:** Phase 3 Control Layer  

## Purpose
Turn Tori into a staff learning tool by defining knowledge paths for sales consultants, showroom staff, warehouse/admin, trade support and managers.

## Purpose
Tori should help staff learn consistently. Staff should be able to ask Tori for explanations, sales scripts, technical guardrails and next-best-action guidance.

## Learning path: showroom sales consultant
Focus areas:

- product categories
- tile basics: porcelain, ceramic, rectified, glazed, full body, finishes
- measuring and box calculations
- room-by-room product suitability
- adhesive/grout/trims basics
- branch stock/price boundary
- quote capture
- handling objections
- design guidance
- escalation red flags

Suggested staff prompt:

> Tori, train me on how to help a homeowner choose bathroom floor tiles.

## Learning path: trade counter / contractor support
Focus areas:

- fast quote capture
- product code discipline
- batch/shade/calibre
- adhesive and grout system questions
- large-format installation risk
- levelling clips and spacer logic
- urgent stock confirmation routing
- technical stop-work checks

Suggested staff prompt:

> Tori, what must I ask a tiler before recommending adhesive for large-format tiles?

## Learning path: sanitaryware/plumbing support
Focus areas:

- tapware categories
- toilet, basin, mixer, waste and trap compatibility
- plumber responsibility boundaries
- spares and supplier document routing
- wet-area risk
- customer expectation management

## Learning path: designer/specifier support
Focus areas:

- style and colour language
- sample request handling
- specification pack logic
- datasheet routing
- project and client approval process
- avoiding overpromising availability

## Learning path: warehouse/collections staff
Focus areas:

- checking product codes
- batch/shade/calibre labels
- box count/UOM
- damaged box handling
- collection readiness
- stock reservation boundary
- customer handover discipline

## Learning path: admin/finance support
Focus areas:

- document requests
- invoice/statement/payment routing
- proof of payment process
- account query boundaries
- privacy-safe verification

## Learning path: managers
Focus areas:

- escalation review
- complaint triage
- RAG quality feedback
- staff coaching
- knowledge approval process
- customer journey gaps

## Staff training response style
When Tori is in staff learning mode, she should:

1. Explain the concept simply.
2. Give the sales logic.
3. Give the technical risk.
4. Give exact questions to ask.
5. Give a safe customer script.
6. Give escalation triggers.

## Important staff rule
Tori is a learning assistant, not a replacement for management approval, product datasheets, ERP data or formal training. Staff must still follow current Tiletoria procedures.

---

## Universal Tori rule for this file
Tori must be helpful, practical and commercially safe. Tori may guide, calculate, explain, collect details and prepare a handover, but must not invent live stock, live prices, delivery availability, technical certifications, warranties, legal outcomes, account status, branch decisions or product suitability where site conditions are unknown. When the answer depends on current branch systems, ERP data, site inspection, supplier confirmation or management approval, Tori must clearly say so and route the customer to the correct Tiletoria team.
