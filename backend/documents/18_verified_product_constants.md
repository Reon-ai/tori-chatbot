# Verified Product Constants

## Use Rule

Use these values only for the named product. Current packaging and the latest manufacturer technical data sheet override this file.

## TAL Screedmaster

- type: self-levelling compound
- approximate consumption: 1.8 kg/m²/mm
- pack size: 22 kg
- published application thickness: 3–16 mm
- official page: https://tal.co.za/screedmaster

## TAL Floor Primer

- approximate coverage: 4–5 m²/L per coat
- coverage depends on substrate porosity and site conditions
- listed pack sizes: 5 L and 25 L
- official page: https://tal.co.za/tal-floorprimer

## TAL Sureproof

- approximate coverage: 10–12 m² per 20 kg kit
- named pack size: 20 kg kit
- reinforcing membrane required at named critical areas according to the system
- official page: https://tal.co.za/tal-sureproof

## TAL Sureproof Shower

- approximate coverage: 3 m² per 5 kg bucket
- published cured film thickness: 1.5–2.0 mm
- named pack size: 5 kg
- official page: https://tal.co.za/tal-sureproof-shower

## TAL Wall & Floor Grout

- published joint range: 2–8 mm
- listed pack sizes: 5 kg and 20 kg
- official page: https://tal.co.za/wall-floor-grout

## TAL Quarrygrout

- published joint range: 5–25 mm
- listed pack sizes: 5 kg and 20 kg
- official page: https://tal.co.za/quarrygrout

## TAL Industrial Epoxy Grout Formula Constant

- published SG in its coverage formula: 1.8
- do not use 1.8 automatically for another grout
- official data sheet: https://tal.co.za/pub/media/product_description/TAL%20INDUSTRIAL%20EPOXY%20GROUT%20-%206.15.pdf

## TAL Profix Plus LVT

Published approximate coverage:

- 1.6 mm V-notch: about 0.63 kg/m² or 10.9 m² per 5 L kit
- 3 mm V-notch: about 0.98 kg/m² or 7.0 m² per 5 L kit
- official product page: https://tal.co.za/tal-profix-plus-lvt

## TAL Large-Format Interior Floor Guidance

The December 2024 TAL guideline states for the named system:

- solid adhesive bed at least 6 mm beneath floor tiles
- back-buttering required for large-format tiles
- interior surface-bed movement joints at maximum 5 m centres
- suspended-slab movement joints at maximum 3 m centres
- movement joints at least 5 mm wide through tile and adhesive
- porcelain and hard-body tile joints minimum 3 mm in that guideline
- glazed ceramic floor tile joints minimum 5 mm in that guideline

Official guideline:

https://tal.co.za/pub/media/Datasheets%202025/INSTALLATION%20GUIDELINE%20-%20FIXING%20LARGE%20FORMAT%20PORCELAIN%2C%20HARD%20BODY%20OR%20CERAMIC%20TILES%20ONTO%20INTERIOR%20WALLS%20%26%20FLOORS%20-%2012.2024.pdf
