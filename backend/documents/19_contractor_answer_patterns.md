# Contractor Question and Answer Patterns

## “How many boxes for 73 m²?”

Ask:

> “What is the exact m² per box? I will use 10% wastage unless your project specifies another allowance.”

## “How many bags of adhesive for 100 m²?”

Ask:

> “Which adhesive and what coverage does the bag state for your trowel and application? Also confirm the tile size and whether the substrate is already flat.”

## “The floor is uneven. Just add more adhesive?”

Answer:

> “No. The unevenness must be measured and the correct levelling or repair system selected. Extra adhesive is not a reliable substitute for substrate preparation.”

## “How much grout for 600 × 600 tiles?”

Ask for:

- tile thickness
- joint width
- area
- grout SG
- pack size

Then use the grout formula.

## “How many bags of self-leveller?”

Ask:

> “What is the floor area, true average thickness, exact product consumption and bag size?”

## “How much waterproofing for a shower?”

Ask for:

- floor size
- total wall length
- waterproofing height
- niches, bench, kerb and returns
- exact product

Do not calculate from floor area only.

## “Do I need movement joints?”

Answer:

> “Yes, the final layout depends on the substrate and system. Is this a surface bed or suspended slab, and what are the room dimensions?”

## “How many wedges do I need?”

Answer:

> “Wedges must cover the maximum number of clips active at one time. Tell me your clip spacing, tile and room dimensions, and how large a section you install before releasing wedges.”

## “Can I tile over this crack?”

Answer:

> “Do not cover it until the crack type and movement are assessed. Please share an overview photo, close-up, crack width, substrate type and whether the crack is changing.”

## “Can grout waterproof the shower?”

Answer:

> “No. The shower requires a compatible waterproofing system behind the tile finish. Grout and adhesive are part of the tile system, not a substitute for waterproofing.”

## “Can you give me the full shopping list?”

Answer with the bill of materials categories in `14_full_project_bom.md`, marking unknown items for confirmation.

## “Can I collect today?”

Answer:

> “I cannot confirm live stock or collection readiness. Please provide the product code, quantity and preferred branch so the team can confirm it.”
