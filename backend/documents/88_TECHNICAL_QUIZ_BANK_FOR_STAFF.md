# Technical Quiz Bank for Staff

**Version:** 2026-06-18  
**Market:** South Africa / Southern Africa  
**Business:** Tiletoria / Tori RAG brain  
**Layer:** Phase 3 Control Layer  

## Purpose
Give Tori a question bank for staff training and self-testing across tile, LVT, installation, sales and risk topics.

## Retrieval triggers
- quiz
- test me
- staff quiz
- training questions
- technical quiz
- sales quiz
- tile quiz
- LVT quiz

## How Tori should use this file
When staff ask for a quiz, Tori should ask 3 to 10 questions at a time, wait for answers, then score and coach. Tori should not overwhelm staff with the full bank unless requested.

## Tile fundamentals quiz
1. What is the difference between porcelain and ceramic tile?
2. What does rectified mean?
3. Why is batch/shade/calibre important?
4. Why should customers buy extra tiles for waste?
5. Why can a polished tile be risky in wet areas?
6. What does shade variation mean?
7. What is a grout joint and why is it needed?
8. Can grout make a shower waterproof?

## Measuring and calculation quiz
1. How do you calculate room m²?
2. A room is 4 m x 3 m. What is the area?
3. If a tile box covers 1.96 m², how many boxes are needed for 20 m² before waste?
4. Why should waste be higher for diagonal or complex layouts?
5. Why must the final box count be rounded up?

## Adhesive and installation quiz
1. Why does substrate preparation matter?
2. What is back-buttering and why is it important for large format?
3. Why must old paint, dust or weak screed be treated before tiling?
4. What site conditions affect adhesive choice?
5. Why must movement joints not be filled with rigid grout?

## Large-format quiz
1. What makes large-format tiles more demanding to install?
2. Why is floor flatness important?
3. Why are levelling clips not a substitute for proper adhesive coverage?
4. Why should lippage concerns be addressed before adhesive cures?
5. What should staff ask before recommending 600x1200 or larger tiles?

## LVT quiz
1. What type of LVT does Tiletoria sell?
2. Why is the subfloor critical for glue-down LVT?
3. What can happen if moisture is trapped under LVT?
4. What is telegraphing?
5. Why should click LVT advice not be given as if Tiletoria sells it?

## Wet area quiz
1. Why is grout not waterproof?
2. What questions must staff ask for a shower floor?
3. Why are balconies high risk?
4. What makes ramps and pool areas safety-sensitive?
5. When should staff escalate wet-area advice?

## Complaint quiz
1. What photos are needed for a damaged goods complaint?
2. What should staff avoid saying when a customer claims a product is defective?
3. Why is it important to ask whether the product is installed?
4. What is the difference between guidance and admitting liability?
5. What is the correct response to a legal threat?

## Sales objection quiz
1. Customer says: "Your price is too high." What is a good response?
2. Customer says: "Tile Africa has it cheaper." What should staff do?
3. Customer says: "Can I buy less waste?" What should staff explain?
4. Customer says: "My tiler says adhesive is not needed for this." How should staff respond?
5. Customer says: "Will this definitely not slip?" What is the safe answer?

## Scoring guide
- 90 to 100 percent: strong
- 70 to 89 percent: good but needs practice
- 50 to 69 percent: needs coaching
- below 50 percent: repeat foundation modules

## Coaching style
Tori should coach constructively:

> Good answer. The missing point is [concept]. A safer customer-facing way to say it is [script].

---

## Universal Tori rule for this file
Tori must be helpful, practical and commercially safe. Tori may guide, calculate, explain, collect details and prepare a handover, but must not invent live stock, live prices, delivery availability, technical certifications, warranties, legal outcomes, account status, branch decisions or product suitability where site conditions are unknown. When the answer depends on current branch systems, ERP data, site inspection, supplier confirmation or management approval, Tori must clearly say so and route the customer to the correct Tiletoria team.
